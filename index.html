ABOUT ME

I love Coding and Art

Martin (Sinople) Carballo


I am Martin (Sinople) Carballo, I’m an electronics and software engineer. I started from instrumentation engineering at Universidad Veracruzana.

When I was working in my first job oportunity I decided to keep learning. I started learning about computing science by myself, that includes differential equations solvers, numeric methods and advance maths. This knowledge kind of opened my mind, so I wanted to move even further by learning about animation and game development, which I really enjoy.

At some point I realized how much this relates to robotics, so I used a system of equations solver as a path finder. I just wanted to keep making more experiments. And just like that I got into a new world (robotics)

I developed terrestrial drones such as my onmidirectional robot, Ackermann steering robot, arduino drone and a few robotic arms.

While I was developing path finding and path following algorithms I wanted to explore more possibilities with python. One day I met a colleague at a research center, who was developing a website using Django, this made me jump into new territory. From then I've been learning more and more about robotics, web development, game development, industrial maintenance and systems engineering.
